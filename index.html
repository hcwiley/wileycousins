hardware design  

& 

development

We have experience with designing PCBs to meet a variety of size and cost requirements.

We have designed circuit boards and developed firmware that controls locks for doors, sets mood lighting, and flys quadcopters.

embedded wireless 

& 

wifi

Communicating over wireless is our favorite. It's magic, and so are we when it comes to wireless.

We have experience with WiFi, sub gHz wireless, and Bluetooth & Bluetooth Low Energy.

android 

& 

iOS apps

"I've got a great idea for an app!" We know, that's why we make them.

We make apps that talk to our hardware, so that you can talk to our hardware. We specialize in apps that talk to nearby devices over WiFi and Bluetooth; as well as to the cloud over cellular data.

physical prototyping 

& 

production

What do you get when put an engineer and a sculptor together?

A whole lot of things. We love making physical things. Whether you need a phone case, mechanical gearing, or a birdhouse we're your guys.
